# Sample Research Digest Active Risks

Status: public sample

## Risks

- The sample may look too abstract if it has no small runtime fixture.
- Public users may confuse AICOS internal actor classes with project-facing
  roles.
- Overly large examples may encourage users to mirror entire source repos into
  AICOS.
- Under-specified writeback rules may lead agents to write continuity into the
  wrong repo.
