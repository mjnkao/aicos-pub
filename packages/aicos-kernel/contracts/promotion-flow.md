# Promotion Flow Contract

Status: initial MVP contract

Promotion is explicit and reviewable:

1. Evidence may be copied into a review packet for `evidence-to-working`.
2. Working material may be copied into a review packet for
   `working-to-canonical`.
3. Human review decides whether the source becomes durable working or canonical
   truth.

The MVP CLI creates review packets. It does not silently rewrite canonical
truth.
